Save The Frogs

We believe that the website design/look and feel, could be improved to bring in more awareness, volunteer interest, and donations for the betterment of amphibian conservation efforts. Based on the usability testing results, it became clear that to improve the UX key flows and refereshing the look, Save The Frogs needs redesigning.

My Role: UX/UI designer Platforms:Mobile,Web Timeline:Three weeks

Research

Based on the results of the survey Web Design was the highest rated category for needing improvement. Affinitizing our user feedback, allowed us to focus on the areas that spoke to users and the areas that could use improvement

Previous Next

Considering the pain points of the users,we reduced the number of pages, consolidated content to prevent redundancy, Decluttered the hero image so that users could focus on sections at a time, and simplified the navigation bar (more inline with industry standard/user expectations).
